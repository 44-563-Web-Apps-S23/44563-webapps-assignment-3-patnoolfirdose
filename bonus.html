Forest Green Penguin

Since no invading people or other animals have ever been found there, the Snares islands are the #1 vacation option for nature lovers in New Zealand. A land of twisted trees resembling Lilliput is ruled over by a breeding colony of woodland penguins. The ferny forest is actually home to a labyrinthine penguin metropolis made up of multiple colonies connected by muddy little highways and well-used pathways. We wanted to capture this incredible event on camera.
